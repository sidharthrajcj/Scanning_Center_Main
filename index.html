Our Services

Every Scan, Done Right With Technology That Delivers Clarity.

1.5 Tesla MRI

High-definition imaging for confident diagnosis. The 1.5T GE MRI scanner provides exceptional imaging and diagnostic capability with faster scanning times and superior image quality.

  • Full-body MRI scanning
  • Neurological and musculoskeletal imaging
  • Cardiac and abdominal imaging
  • Contrast and non-contrast studies

Advanced Ultrasound

Mindray Resonai i9 Elite Ultrasound with multiple high-resolution probes, delivering premium image clarity for precise and reliable examinations.

  • Abdominal and pelvic ultrasound
  • Obstetric and fetal ultrasound
  • Vascular and Doppler studies
  • Small parts and musculoskeletal ultrasound
  • 4D ultrasound for expecting parents

Fetal Medicine

Comprehensive care for expecting mothers including detailed fetal anomaly scans, growth monitoring, and specialized consultations.

  • First trimester screening
  • Anomaly scans (level I & II)
  • Fetal echocardiography
  • Doppler studies for fetal wellbeing
  • Genetic counseling

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive diagnostic lab for routine and specialized blood, urine and biochemical tests, guided by experienced professionals.

  • Complete blood count (CBC)
  • Biochemistry and hormonal assays
  • Urine and stool analysis
  • Serology and infectious disease testing
  • Specialized tests and profiles

Nerve Conduction Studies (NCS)

Mapping nerve function for targeted treatment of neurological conditions like carpal tunnel syndrome, peripheral neuropathies, and radiculopathies.

  • Motor and sensory nerve conduction
  • Late responses (F-wave, H-reflex)
  • Repetitive nerve stimulation
  • Blink reflex studies

Electroencephalogram (EEG)

Monitoring brain activity with precision and care for diagnosis of epilepsy, sleep disorders, encephalopathies, and other neurological conditions.

  • Routine EEG (20-30 minutes)
  • Sleep-deprived EEG
  • Long-term video EEG monitoring
  • Ambulatory EEG

Patient Instructions

Come Prepared, Stay Comfortable

MRI Scan

  • Arrival: Please reach 20-30 minutes early for preparation. If oral contrast is needed, arrive 45 minutes early (you will be informed during booking).
  • Pacemakers & Implants: Inform us if you have pacemakers, valves, ICDs, pumps, stents, clips, or metal implants. Some devices are not MRI-safe.
  • Claustrophobia: Our wide-bore MRI, calming lights, music, and continuous communication help reduce anxiety. A companion can stay inside for support.
  • Clothing: Wear loose and comfortable clothes. All metal items (jewellery, phone, belts, wallet, cards) must be removed.

Ultrasound

  • Drink 3-4 glasses of water for abdominal scans.
  • No fasting needed unless told otherwise.

Fetal Scans

  • Bring your doctor's referral.
  • Best done between 12-22 weeks depending on scan type.
